About

Tech-enthusiast that loves learning new things.

I spent a large part of my life being passionate about technology. I thought that technical competencies were everything. Then I realized that communication and storytelling are key.

As matter of fact, I started to develop my communication skills and, since I am a learning-by-doing person, I decided to open this Blog. The objective is to improve communication and learning processes, and, hopefully, help and inspire someone else.

Someone defines me as a super curious person, and I think this is why, in this blog, you can find articles on different topics.

By the way, I am Italian. I choose to write this blog in English to increase my English skills as well.

PS: this Blog has been proudly created by me using Pelican and Bootstrap.
